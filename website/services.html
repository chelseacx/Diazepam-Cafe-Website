DELIEVERY DETAILS


1. When will the goods be delivered?

We will strive to deliver within 2 working days of your purchase. Delivery will be made anytime between Mon to Fri, 10am-5pm and on Sat, 10am-3pm. No delivery on Sundays and Public Holidays.

2. What are the shipping charges?

FREE Delivery for Orders above $100.

Delivery charges for Orders below $100 must be $30.

3. Where can deliveries be made?

Diazepam Cafe is proud to say we ship our products to most homes and business establishments throughout Singapore including Sentosa island.

4 .What happens if I miss my delivery?

We understand that unforeseen circumstances may force you to miss your delivery. If this has happened to you please do contact us to make another arrangement. Re-delivery is chargeable at $8.50.

5. How do I pay for my purchases?

As of now we offer two types of payment options: Paypal and Cash on Delivery. Please indicate your preferred method upon check-out. Prices include GST.
