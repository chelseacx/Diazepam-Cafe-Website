Frequently Asked Questions

General
Q. Are your products suitable for vegetarians/vegans?
A. Our products contain animal products such as milk and eggs. They may not be suitable for vegetarians and cannot be consumed by vegans. However, feel free to drop us an email for any special orders.
Online Orders
Q. How long will it take for my orders to arrive?
A. It depends on the products you have ordered. If they are in stock they will be delivered out on the next working day. Otherwise we will send you an email with the estimated delivery time, typically around 3 to 5 working days. Feel free to check with us on stock availability before placing an online order.
